Jase Money

As the original founding member of Team Gang Squad and one of the fathers of Indigo League, Jase Money is one of SoundCloud’s rare underground gems. Known to many as one of the pioneers of a variety of different sub-genres, he describes his sound as “Experimental Trap”. As audio engineer and team manager, you can contact him about getting any features or bookings with Team Gang Squad. Jase is also known for his production for many credible artists such as Yung Bruh (Lil Tracy), Lord Apex, Shofu, TrippyThaKid, Beamon, & more.

       


YUNG TYMZ

Tymz aka Daytyme aka Yung Tymz is one of the original founding members of the squad. As creator of the infamous name and one of the leading personalities, Tymz heads the mixtapes with his unique and renowned talent in the art of freestyle rapping and singing. Born in Valhalla, NY he moved to North Carolina in 2003 and met Jase Money on the school bus in 2008. He is currently working and living between Louisville, KY and Charlotte, NC. Tymz is known for his collabs with notable artists such as Hurricane Chris, Chevy Woods (Taylor Gang), K-Figgz, TrippyThaKid, Kold-Blooded (Schema-Posse), & many others.


JP

Hailing from Cleveland, Ohio, JP culminates the modern version of a triple threat as an entrepreneur, moving picture artist, and lyricist. As an original local member of Team Gang Squad, he has established himself as a relentless MC and personal videographer for the group's video production. He also heads the cannabis media company ‘Cannabias Media,’ featuring TGS in the premier of his short film titled ‘Grassrootz’. JP has a technical rap style, and when creating songs for his personal projects he propels himself as a conscious MC.
